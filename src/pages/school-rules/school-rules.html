School Rules

Rules

1. Students must be registered before they can attend the Saturday Islamic or Quran (Maktab) Classes.

2. Students must follow the Aadaab of the Masjid and the instruction of teachers and staff.

3. There is a zero tolerance policy for violent behavior. Students will be sent home immediately and further disciplinary action, including expulsion, will be taken. A meeting with the parents will be required before the student is allowed to return.

5. No video games, music players, games, cards or toys, etc are allowed in the Masjid. All such items will be confiscated and returned to the parent. All cell phones must be turned off in the Masjid. There is no cell phone usage allowed in the Masjid.

5. Students are not allowed to play basketball, football, cricket, or any other sport or games during class time. If students are caught playing with any balls, they will be confiscated and returned to the parent.

6. Lunch or snacks will not be normally provided. Send snacks that do not make noise or a mess and water, no juices are allowed.

7. Students should wear proper Islamic Attire to the Masjid. For example salwar kameez or thobe and topi for boys and hijab and abaya for girls. No shorts, clothing with images or inappropriate language, or tight fitting clothing is permitted. Also, the girls should refrain from wearing jewelry, nail polish and perfume.

8. For Saturday Classes, students MUST BE in their class by 10:00 am. Students must remain in class for the entire duration of the Saturday Classes.

9. For Quraan (Maktab) Classes, students MUST BE in their class 10 minutes prior to the time assigned by their instructor for Quraan (Maktab) Classes. Students must remain with their instructor for the entire duration of their assigned time.

10. Quraan (Maktab) Class students will only be instructed during their assigned time. No extra time or accommodation will be given to students who come late or early.

11. Disciplinary action will be taken for those who are consistently late.

12. Students must bring their books and supplies with them for every class. We do not have extras.

13. Students will be assigned to classes according to their knowledge and not their age.

14. Parents are not allowed to sit in the classes.

15. Parents should check for homework and notices and make sure the students complete their homework.

16. Parents should help their children with homework, reviewing the lessons and learning the material.

17. Parents should encourage the children to implement what they have learned. Parents should create a conducive environment at home to help the child in their studies and implement what they learn.

18. Parents must review and sign report cards weekly for Saturday Classes and daily for Quraan Classes.

19. Parents should encourage, reward and reprimand their children, when necessary.

20. Parents should remain in constant contact with the teachers and the school about the performance and behavior of their children.

21. Fathers should accompany sons to the Masjid for 5 times salah and programs, especially on the weekends.

22. Parents must attend the mandatory parent’s meetings and individual parent-teacher conferences.

23. If any parents have any concerns about any of our staff members/teachers, they should contact the Dean of Discipline.

24. Dismissal is at 1:15 pm for Saturday Classes. No teacher or volunteer will be responsible for the children after that time or stay with the children at the Masjid. Parents are requested to pick their children up promptly.

25. Parents MUST pick their children up after the child’s assigned time for Quraan (Maktab) Classes is over. No teacher or volunteer will be responsible for the children after that time or watch the children and the teachers or volunteers will NOT be responsible for keeping the children in the Masjid or keep them from going outside after that time.

26. A note from the parent is required for early dismissal and absences.

27. Girls are not allowed to wait in the parking lot.
